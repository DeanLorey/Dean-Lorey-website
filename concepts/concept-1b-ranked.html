Writer · Co-Creator · Showrunner · Author

Comedy with teeth.
Monsters with heart.

Dean Lorey has been writing for audiences who like their laughs dark and their horror funny since 1993 — cult features, animated superhero hits, and a stack of books for kids who love being a little scared. Co-creator and showrunner of Max's Harley Quinn, showrunner of James Gunn's Creature Commandos, author of the award-winning Nightmare Academy trilogy.
