About

Georgia kid. L.A. writer. Monster enthusiast.

I grew up in Conyers, Georgia, loving comics and horror movies. I moved to L.A. at 23, co-wrote Major Payne, spent a decade in sitcom writers' rooms, and somehow ended up running animated DC shows.

These days I split my time between Harley Quinn, Creature Commandos, and whatever book is currently refusing to get written.
